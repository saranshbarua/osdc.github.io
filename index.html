About us

We are the Open Source Community of Jaypee Institute of Information Technology based in Noida, India. A community of web developers, android freaks, machine learning enthusiasts, designers, game developers and most significantly EXPLORERS. We welcome those who believe in open source philosophy and who are willing to sacrifice their naps in order to change the world by their code.

For the sake of encouraging more people to lean into the open source world we organise workshops on regular basis based on new and trending technologies. We also organise events such as bootcamps, late night hackathons, and a mega hackathon on our college's annual fest. We love having late night conversations on tech and building new things. If you love the same just hop in, we are looking forward for your participation.
